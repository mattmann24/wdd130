Half Day Trip

This is our half day trip. This route was made in mind of those who are new to white water rafting. We wanted to have a friendly, and exhilerating experience. This is perfect for travelers who have never been, or who have limited time but love the water.

Full Day Trip

With our Full Day trip, you get to enjoy the river for longer. This is an extension of our half day. It also will have more intense rapids. perfect for intermediate rafters.

Three Day Trip

Our Three Day Trip is the pinical of our white water rafting experience. It may be a larger amount of time, but this allows you to go to our more secluded sections of our river. Guide Info and Trip Expectations

Half Day

Since this day is designed for a more relaxed trip we have flexible options. With beginners we recommend 1 guide per boat. But more can be arranged. With an experience group. 1 guide per party. Prices will very depending on the party size.

Full Day

A Full day is a 8 hour trip and we want to make sure we have a full team on hand, this will require 1 guide per boat regardless of skill or experience.

Three Day

This Trip will require a planning meeting with our lead guide. With his experience, this meeting will be easy, but we need to assess each party and their needs accordingly. Plans, preperations, and check ins are in place to help confirm the readyiness will be required. But this extra work is well worth it! This ensures everyone has a safe and amazing adventure.
